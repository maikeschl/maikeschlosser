Welcome

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Economics at the University of Konstanz, Germany, supervised by Prof. Christina Felfe (Chair of Applied Microeconomics).

My research interests lie in behavioral microeconomics, with a particular focus on topics related to education, discrimination, and law and economics.

In 2025, I received the University of Konstanz Award for Teaching Excellence (LUKS) of the Department of Economics.

I was a visiting Ph.D. student with Elif Kubilay (University of Essex) in 2024 and at Aix-Marseille University in 2025.
